Our Proven Process

A Systematic Approach to Building Your Dream Home

Your Journey with Deacon Construction

Phase 1

Discovery & Planning

Months 1-3

Initial Consultation

Duration: 2-3 hours | Location: Our office or your lot

We begin with an in-depth conversation about your vision, lifestyle, budget, and timeline. This consultation includes:

  • Detailed discussion of your needs and wants
  • Site evaluation and analysis
  • Preliminary budget discussion
  • Review of our process and timeline
  • Initial design concepts and inspiration
You'll receive: Project feasibility assessment and next steps outline

Site Analysis & Surveys

Duration: 2-3 weeks | Investment: Varies by site

Professional site evaluation to understand opportunities and constraints:

  • Topographical survey
  • Soil and geotechnical analysis
  • Setback and easement verification
  • Utility availability assessment
  • Environmental considerations
You'll receive: Comprehensive site analysis report with recommendations

Design Development

Duration: 6-8 weeks | Investment: Design fee applies

Collaborative design process with our team or your chosen architect:

  • Conceptual design development
  • 3D renderings and visualizations
  • Floor plan refinement
  • Exterior elevation design
  • Preliminary material selections
You'll receive: Detailed design package with 3D renderings
Phase 2

Pre-Construction

Months 3-6

Construction Documents

Duration: 4-6 weeks | Responsibility: Architect/Designer

Detailed architectural and engineering drawings for construction and permits:

  • Complete architectural drawings
  • Structural engineering plans
  • MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) designs
  • Site and landscape plans
  • Detailed specifications
You'll receive: Complete construction document set

Permits & Approvals

Duration: 4-8 weeks | Responsibility: Deacon Construction

We handle all permit applications and regulatory approvals:

  • Building permit application
  • Septic system permits
  • Well permits (if applicable)
  • Driveway permits
  • HOA or architectural review board approvals
You'll receive: All permits and approvals in hand

Final Contract & Pricing

Duration: 1-2 weeks | Investment: Contract deposit required

Detailed contract with transparent pricing and specifications:

  • Line-item construction cost breakdown
  • Material allowances and specifications
  • Construction timeline and milestones
  • Change order procedures
  • Warranty terms and conditions
You'll receive: Signed construction contract and timeline
Phase 3

Construction

Months 6-18

Foundation & Structure

Duration: 6-8 weeks | Milestone: Foundation completion

Site preparation and structural foundation work:

  • Site clearing and preparation
  • Excavation and grading
  • Foundation and basement construction
  • Structural framing
  • Roof system installation
Client involvement: Weekly progress reports and site visits

Systems Installation

Duration: 4-6 weeks | Milestone: Rough-in complete

Installation of all home systems and utilities:

  • Plumbing rough-in
  • Electrical rough-in
  • HVAC system installation
  • Insulation and vapor barriers
  • Windows and exterior doors
Client involvement: Systems walkthrough and selections confirmation

Interior Finishes

Duration: 8-10 weeks | Milestone: Interior completion

Installation of all interior finishes and fixtures:

  • Drywall and interior paint
  • Flooring installation
  • Kitchen and bathroom cabinetry
  • Countertops and backsplashes
  • Interior trim and millwork
  • Lighting and plumbing fixtures
Client involvement: Regular selection appointments and progress reviews
Phase 4

Completion & Delivery

Month 18+

Final Details & Cleanup

Duration: 2-3 weeks | Focus: Perfect finishing

Attention to every detail for a perfect finish:

  • Final paint touch-ups
  • Hardware and fixture adjustments
  • Appliance installation and testing
  • Landscaping and exterior cleanup
  • Deep cleaning and final preparations
Client involvement: Pre-walkthrough punch list creation

Final Inspections & Approvals

Duration: 1 week | Milestone: Certificate of occupancy

Official inspections and approvals for occupancy:

  • Final building inspection
  • Certificate of occupancy
  • Final utility connections
  • System testing and commissioning
  • Quality assurance review
You'll receive: All certificates and approvals for occupancy

Final Walkthrough & Delivery

Duration: 2-3 hours | Milestone: Key delivery

Comprehensive walkthrough and home delivery:

  • Complete home walkthrough
  • Systems operation training
  • Warranty documentation review
  • Maintenance schedule and recommendations
  • Key delivery and access codes
You'll receive: Keys to your completed dream home!
