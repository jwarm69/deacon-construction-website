Our Custom Home Building Process

A proven approach refined over 25 years of luxury home construction

1. Discovery & Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle needs, budget, and timeline. We'll discuss your lot, review any existing plans, and establish the foundation for your project.

  • Complimentary initial consultation
  • Lot evaluation and site analysis
  • Budget and timeline discussion
  • Preliminary design concepts

2. Design & Planning

Working with our design team or your chosen architect, we develop detailed plans that maximize your lot's potential while reflecting your personal style and functional requirements.

  • Collaborative design development
  • 3D renderings and visualizations
  • Material and finish selections
  • Permit acquisition and approvals

3. Contract & Pre-Construction

Once plans are finalized, we provide a detailed contract with transparent pricing. Our pre-construction phase ensures every detail is planned before we break ground.

  • Detailed cost breakdown
  • Material specifications and allowances
  • Construction timeline
  • Final permit approvals

4. Construction

Our experienced craftsmen bring your home to life with meticulous attention to detail. We maintain open communication throughout construction with regular updates and site visits.

  • Regular progress updates
  • Quality control inspections
  • Client walkthrough opportunities
  • Expert craftsman execution

5. Completion & Beyond

Your final walkthrough ensures every detail meets our exacting standards. We provide comprehensive warranty support and remain available for any future needs.

  • Detailed final inspection
  • Comprehensive warranty coverage
  • Home systems orientation
  • Ongoing support and maintenance

Custom Home FAQs

How long does it take to build a custom home?

Typically 12-18 months from groundbreaking to completion, depending on size and complexity. The design and permitting phase adds another 3-6 months to the total timeline.

Can you help us find the right lot?

Absolutely! We have extensive knowledge of available lots in the Cashiers area and can help you find the perfect site for your dream home, considering factors like views, building constraints, and value.

Do you work with our architect?

Yes! We work with many talented local architects and are happy to collaborate with your chosen design professional. We can also recommend architects we've successfully partnered with.

What's included in your pricing?

Our pricing includes all construction, standard fixtures and finishes, permits, and site preparation. We provide detailed allowances for selections and upgrades during the planning phase.

How do you handle cost overruns?

We provide detailed contracts and regular budget updates. Any changes are discussed and approved before implementation. Our experience and planning process minimize surprises.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer comprehensive warranty coverage on structural elements, systems, and craftsmanship. Details are provided during our consultation, and we stand behind our work long-term.
