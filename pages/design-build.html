Our Integrated Design-Build Process

Experience the advantage of unified design and construction expertise

01

Discovery & Programming

2-4 weeks

  • Initial consultation and project goals
  • Site analysis and feasibility study
  • Budget parameters and timeline
  • Lifestyle and functional requirements
  • Preliminary program development
Deliverables: Project program, site analysis, preliminary budget
02

Schematic Design

3-5 weeks

  • Conceptual design development
  • Space planning and layout options
  • Preliminary structural systems
  • Cost modeling and value engineering
  • Design refinement with client feedback
Deliverables: Schematic drawings, 3D renderings, budget refinement
03

Design Development

4-6 weeks

  • Detailed architectural drawings
  • Structural and MEP coordination
  • Material and finish selections
  • Landscape design integration
  • Guaranteed maximum price (GMP) development
Deliverables: Design development drawings, material specifications, GMP
04

Construction Documents

4-6 weeks

  • Complete construction drawings
  • Technical specifications
  • Permit application preparation
  • Final material and equipment selections
  • Construction contract finalization
Deliverables: Complete construction documents, permits, signed contract
05

Pre-Construction

2-4 weeks

  • Final permit approval
  • Subcontractor selection and contracts
  • Material ordering and lead times
  • Site preparation and access
  • Construction scheduling
Deliverables: Permits, subcontractor agreements, project schedule
06

Construction

8-14 months

  • Site preparation and foundation
  • Structural framing and systems
  • Interior and exterior finishes
  • Quality control and inspections
  • Regular client communication
Deliverables: Weekly progress reports, milestone inspections, completed home
