Our Commitment to Cashiers

For over 15 years, Deacon Construction has been more than a home builder in Cashiers - we've been a community partner, environmental steward, and local advocate.

We believe that building exceptional homes is just the beginning. Our responsibility extends to preserving the natural beauty that makes Western North Carolina special, supporting local organizations that strengthen our community, and fostering the values that make Cashiers a place people want to call home.

Sustainable Building Practices

Supporting Local Business

Strengthening our local economy through partnerships

Local First Commitment

We believe a strong local economy creates a strong community. That's why we prioritize partnerships with local businesses and craftspeople whenever possible.

85% Local subcontractors
90% Regional material sourcing
50+ Local business partnerships

How Local Partnerships Benefit Our Community:

  • Creates and maintains local jobs
  • Keeps construction dollars in the local economy
  • Supports traditional mountain crafts and trades
  • Reduces transportation costs and environmental impact
  • Builds long-term relationships with trusted partners

Featured Local Partners

Mountain Stone Masonry

Three generations of stone craftsmen creating beautiful fireplaces and facades using local fieldstone.

Carolina Custom Millwork

Custom cabinetry and millwork crafted from locally harvested hardwoods and traditional joinery techniques.

Blue Ridge Landscaping

Native plant specialists creating sustainable landscapes that enhance and preserve our mountain environment.
