Meet Our Team

The Master Craftsmen Behind Your Dream Home

Excellence Through Expertise

Behind every exceptional home stands a team of dedicated professionals who share a passion for quality craftsmanship and client satisfaction. Meet the skilled artisans, designers, and project managers who bring your vision to life.

At Deacon Construction, we believe that building your dream home should be a collaborative and enjoyable experience. Our team combines decades of experience with innovative techniques and unwavering attention to detail to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Leadership Team

Kevin Cler

Founder & President

With over 25 years of experience in luxury home construction, Kevin Cler has established Deacon Construction as the premier custom home builder in Western North Carolina. His vision of combining traditional mountain craftsmanship with modern luxury has resulted in over 150 exceptional homes throughout the Cashiers area.

Kevin's hands-on approach means he personally oversees every project from initial consultation to final walkthrough. His commitment to quality and client satisfaction has earned him recognition from industry peers and a loyal following of satisfied homeowners.

A Cashiers resident for over 30 years, Kevin understands the unique challenges and opportunities of mountain construction. His deep knowledge of local building codes, suppliers, and site conditions ensures every project is completed to the highest standards.
