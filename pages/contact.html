Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a custom home?

Typically 12-18 months from ground breaking to completion, depending on size and complexity. The design and permitting phase adds another 3-6 months.

Do you work with clients' architects?

Absolutely! We work with many talented local architects and are happy to collaborate with your chosen design professional.

What's included in your initial consultation?

We'll discuss your vision, budget, timeline, and lot details. If you have plans, we'll review them. The consultation is free and typically lasts 1-2 hours.

How do you handle cost overruns?

We provide detailed contracts and regular updates. Any changes are discussed and approved before implementation. Our experience helps minimize surprises.

Can you help with lot selection?

Yes! We have extensive knowledge of the area and can help you find the perfect lot for your dream home, considering factors like views, building constraints, and value.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer a comprehensive warranty covering structural elements, systems, and craftsmanship. Our reputation depends on your long-term satisfaction.
