Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about building in the mountains

How long does it take to build a custom mountain home?

Typical custom mountain homes take 10-14 months from groundbreaking to completion. Our design-build process often reduces this by 20-30% compared to traditional methods. Factors affecting timeline include home size, complexity, weather, and permit processing times.

What's the average cost per square foot in Cashiers?

Luxury custom homes in the Cashiers area typically range from $350-$600+ per square foot, depending on finishes, complexity, and site conditions. Mountain terrain, access challenges, and premium materials can affect costs. We provide detailed cost breakdowns during the planning phase.

What building restrictions exist in mountain areas?

Mountain communities often have height restrictions, setback requirements, and architectural guidelines to preserve views and character. Some areas require specific materials or colors. We handle all permitting and ensure compliance with local regulations.

Can you build year-round in the mountains?

Yes, though winter weather can slow certain activities like concrete work and exterior finishes. We plan construction schedules to maximize productivity during favorable weather and adjust timelines accordingly. Indoor work continues through winter months.

How do you handle utilities and infrastructure?

We coordinate all utility connections including power, water, sewer/septic, internet, and gas. Mountain lots may require well water, septic systems, or extended utility runs. We assess all infrastructure needs during site evaluation and include costs in project planning.

What makes mountain construction different?

Mountain construction requires expertise in steep terrain, drainage management, foundation challenges, and extreme weather conditions. We use specialized equipment, materials rated for mountain climates, and techniques proven in mountain environments.
