Renovation FAQs

Can we live in our home during renovation?

In most cases, yes! We plan projects in phases to minimize disruption. Kitchen and bathroom renovations may require temporary alternatives, but we work to keep living spaces functional.

How long do renovations typically take?

Timeline varies by scope: kitchen renovations 6-12 weeks, bathroom renovations 4-8 weeks, whole home renovations 4-8 months. We provide detailed schedules during planning.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all permits, inspections, and regulatory approvals. Our local knowledge ensures compliance with Cashiers building codes and HOA requirements.

What about unexpected issues during renovation?

Our thorough initial assessment minimizes surprises. When unexpected issues arise, we discuss options and pricing before proceeding, maintaining transparency throughout.

Can you match existing materials and finishes?

Yes, we excel at matching existing materials or finding suitable alternatives. Our craftsmen can replicate historic details and architectural elements seamlessly.

Do renovations increase home value?

Quality renovations typically increase home value, especially in the Cashiers market. We focus on improvements that provide both immediate enjoyment and long-term value.
