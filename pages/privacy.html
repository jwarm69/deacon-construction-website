Overview

At Deacon Construction LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our services.

By using our website or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our website or services.

Quick Summary

  • We only collect information necessary to provide our services
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • You have control over your personal information

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, such as:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Project Information: Details about your construction or renovation project, budget ranges, timeline preferences
  • Communication Records: Messages, emails, phone call notes, and meeting records
  • Financial Information: Payment information for contracted services (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Property Information: Details about your property, lot specifications, and construction site information

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, referral sources
  • Location Data: General geographic location (city/region level) based on IP address
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: See our Cookies section below for details

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Referral partners and clients who recommend our services
  • Public records related to property ownership and permits
  • Professional associations and licensing boards
  • Social media platforms when you interact with our content

How We Use Your Information

Primary Business Purposes

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Plan, design, and construct your custom home or renovation project
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries, provide project updates, and maintain client relationships
  • Project Management: Coordinate subcontractors, suppliers, and project timelines
  • Quality Assurance: Document project progress and ensure quality standards
  • Financial Management: Process payments, manage contracts, and handle billing

Marketing and Business Development

With your consent, we may use your information to:

  • Send newsletters and updates about our services
  • Invite you to company events and home tours
  • Request testimonials and reviews
  • Feature your project in our portfolio (with explicit permission)
  • Provide information about additional services that may interest you

Legal and Compliance

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain proper business records and documentation
  • Respond to legal requests and proceedings
  • Protect our rights and interests

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Service Providers and Business Partners

We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in providing services:

  • Subcontractors: Licensed professionals working on your project
  • Suppliers: Material and equipment vendors
  • Professional Services: Architects, engineers, and consultants
  • Technology Providers: Website hosting, email services, project management software
  • Financial Services: Payment processors and lending partners

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, clients, or others
  • Investigate or prevent fraud or security issues
  • Enforce our terms of service or contracts

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

What We Don't Do

We never:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information for others' marketing purposes without consent
  • Use your information for purposes unrelated to our business relationship

Data Security

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure Storage: Physical and digital security measures for document storage
  • Regular Updates: Software and security systems are regularly updated
  • Employee Training: Staff are trained on privacy and security best practices

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing ongoing services and support
  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolving disputes and enforcing agreements
  • Business record-keeping (typically 7-10 years for construction projects)

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Immediately investigate and contain the incident
  • Notify affected individuals and authorities as required by law
  • Take steps to prevent future occurrences
  • Provide assistance and support to affected individuals

Cookies and Analytics

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage and improve user experience
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Track website visits for marketing attribution (with consent)

Third-Party Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage. Google Analytics may collect information about your visits and use of our website. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your information to another service provider
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your information
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your information

Marketing Communications

You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Contacting us directly
  • Updating your preferences in our client portal

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days and may need to verify your identity for security purposes.
