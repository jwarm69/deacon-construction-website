Our Story

Building Dreams in the Mountains Since 1999

A Legacy of Excellence in Mountain Home Building

For over 25 years, Deacon Construction LLC has been transforming visions into architectural masterpieces throughout the stunning landscapes of Cashiers and Western North Carolina.

Founded by Kevin Cler in 1999, Deacon Construction emerged from a simple belief: that building luxury homes in the mountains requires not just skill and craftsmanship, but a deep understanding of how architecture can honor and enhance the natural beauty of our region.

What began as a small operation focused on quality over quantity has grown into one of the most respected custom home builders in Western North Carolina. Yet despite our growth, we've never lost sight of what made us successful: an unwavering commitment to excellence, personalized service, and building homes that stand as testaments to both our clients' dreams and our craftsmanship.

Kevin Cler, Founder & President
